Our Mission

Our mission is to put the power back into people's voices. We all have a right to participate in politics, know what is happening, and voice our opinions. Unfortunately, this is a right that many of us have given up on. In its current state, politics is exhausting and divisive, and it is hard to know if your voice is being listened to.

We believe that politics should be accessible to all.
